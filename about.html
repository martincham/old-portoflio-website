I am a third year Studio Art and Computer Science major at Grinnell College. I work in sculptural work in woodworking, casting, ceramics, welding and more. I am highly interested in the physical processes of these crafts and finding ways to make things in a thorough, efficient, and modular manner. Outside of artistic practice I am interested in the intersection of aesthetics and functionality: user interface design, graphic design, physical object design, etc.
